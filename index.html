HTML BLOCK-LEVEL AND INLINE ELEMENTS

Block-level elements

Blocklevel elements : are elements that automatically starts on a new line.

10 examples of block-level elements:

address , dd , dt ,table, ol , ul ,blockquote, main, div, header.

Inline elements

These are elements that do not start on a new line. It only takes as much width as necessary

10 examples of inline elements :

b (for bold) ,a(for hyperlink) ,abbr(abbreviation) ,button , big, small, strong, time, span, em(embed).
